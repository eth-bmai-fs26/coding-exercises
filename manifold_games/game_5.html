Key Insight — The Inverse Map φ⁻¹
Recovering u (big angle)
θ = atan2(Y, X)
u = θ / (2π)
u encodes which sector of the ring the pod is in. atan2 recovers the angle from (X, Y).
Recovering v (tube angle)
ρ = √(X²+Y²) − R
v = atan2(Z, ρ) / (2π)
v encodes how far around the tube the pod is. ρ is the signed radial offset from the ring center.
Full inverse
φ⁻¹(X,Y,Z) =
(atan2(Y,X)/(2π),
 atan2(Z,ρ−R)/(2π))
Both angles are mod 1 (wrap to [0,1]). Two atan2 calls recover both parameters uniquely.
